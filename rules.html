Blindsoft Server Rules
Blindsoft Enterprises Server

Welcome to the Blindsoft Discord server! To keep our community safe and enjoyable for everyone, please follow the rules listed below. By participating here, you agree to these guidelines.

Basic Server Rules

  1. No spamming. Bot commands may be used in the command prompt channel.
  2. No hate speech. This includes racism, sexism, ableism, bigotry, or any form of discrimination.
  3. NSFW content is strictly prohibited. Offenders will be permanently banned, as users under 18 are present.
  4. Do not use alternate accounts to bypass bans. These accounts (and possibly your main) will be banned immediately.
  5. No malicious content. Malware, spyware, adware, and similar will result in a permanent ban. No doxxing, hacking, or scamming.
  6. Use channels for their intended purposes. For example, roleplaying is not allowed in the main chat.
  7. Follow the Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. Users under 13 will be banned until they meet the age requirement.

Voice Rules

  1. No blasting loud music or "ear rape" content.
  2. Be respectful to all participants in voice calls/chats.
  3. Explicit music is allowed, but keep it clean when possible. Absolutely no pedophilic content—this will result in an immediate ban.
Tip: If a song has a clean or instrumental version, please consider playing that.

Rules for Staff

Note: All server rules apply to staff members.
  1. All staff must enable multifactor authentication to use moderation tools (kick, ban, etc.).
  2. Do not add bots without Adison’s consent. If Adison is unavailable, contact a trusted installer. Unverified bots may be removed automatically for security reasons. Contact Adison Verlice for bot approval.
  3. Do not attempt to delete logs or moderation logs. Doing so will result in immediate removal from staff roles.
  4. Do not use your staff role to help users bypass or violate punishments. This leads to instant demotion, unless it was a genuine mistake (e.g., misidentifying content).
  5. All punishments must have a valid reason. Lack of justification may result in demotion, temporary or permanent.
  6. Never mention @everyone without Adison’s explicit consent. During lockdowns, only administrators may do so.
  7. Do not manage channels, roles, etc., unless explicitly permitted by Adison. Abuse of permissions will result in a permanent demotion, even in cases of account compromise. This includes excessive bans, webhooks, and other risky actions.
  8. Do not override actions taken by bots unless you are sure it’s a false positive and have approval from a trusted installer. Trusted installers are not obligated to approve your actions if they don’t trust your judgment.
  9. Do not attempt to overrule decisions made by higher roles. If a trusted installer or higher tells you to stop, comply, or face consequences appropriate to the severity.
  10. Be vigilant about account security. Compromised staff accounts used to raid the server will be permanently demoted and cannot be trusted again.

Rule Changes

If you want a rule changed, contact Adison Verlice directly via Discord @ncladisonverlice or via email.

Punishments

Unless otherwise specified above, standard punishments for rule violations include:

  1. Warning: You will receive a DM. If DMs are blocked, you will be mentioned in the relevant channel.
  2. Timeout: You may be timed out for a day.
  3. Temporary ban: Staff may issue bans ranging from 3 hours to 30 days.
  4. Permanent ban: Severe cases result in permanent removal from the server.
